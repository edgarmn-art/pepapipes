Our Story

From Chokaa Roots
to Nairobi's Most
Trusted Hardware

Since 2014, PEPA PIPES has been the hardware store that Chokaa residents, contractors, and developers have relied on. Right next to Muindi Mweusi Supermarket, we've grown from a neighbourhood shop into one of the most recognised building supply destinations along the Eastern Bypass.

When you walk through our doors, you're not just buying materials — you're working with a team that takes your project personally. Our customers look at their finished homes and smile. That's the standard we hold ourselves to every single day.
